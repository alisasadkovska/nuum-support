Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

NUUM is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI fashion stylist app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Images

When you use NUUM to analyze outfits:

  • Outfit Photos: Photos you upload or capture are temporarily processed by our AI system
  • Storage: If you create an account, photos are stored securely in the cloud (Firebase Storage)
  • Guest Mode: Without an account, photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Deletion: You can delete any photo or analysis at any time

1.2 Account Information

If you create an account:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Profile Data: Optional display name and preferences
  • Authentication: Managed securely through Firebase Authentication

1.3 Usage Data

  • Analysis History: Saved outfit analyses with ratings and feedback
  • Preferences: Language selection, theme preferences
  • Subscription Status: Whether you're using free or Pro tier
  • Device Information: Device ID for managing free daily tries (stored locally)

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage: Features used, screens visited (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to improve app stability
  • Device Type: iPhone or iPad model for optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • AI Analysis: Process photos through our AI system (OpenAI GPT-4 Vision) to provide fashion feedback
  • Save History: Store your analyses for future reference
  • Track Usage: Manage free daily tries and subscription benefits
  • Sync Across Devices: Keep your data consistent (if signed in)

2.2 Communication

  • Support: Respond to your questions and issues
  • Updates: Important app updates or policy changes (optional)
  • No Marketing: We do not send promotional emails without explicit consent

2.3 Improvement

  • App Quality: Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Bug Fixes: Identify and fix issues
  • AI Accuracy: Improve fashion analysis quality (anonymized data only)

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 OpenAI (GPT-4 Vision)

  • Purpose: AI-powered outfit analysis
  • Data Shared: Outfit photos are sent to OpenAI for analysis
  • Privacy: OpenAI processes images according to their privacy policy
  • Retention: OpenAI does not retain images used for analysis
  • Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

3.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, cloud storage, database
  • Data Stored: Account info, photos, analysis history
  • Security: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Location: Stored in secure Google Cloud servers
  • Policy: Firebase Privacy

3.3 Apple (App Store, StoreKit)

  • Purpose: In-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Shared: Purchase transactions handled by Apple
  • Privacy: Subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

4.1 Protection Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase Security: Industry-standard security rules and authentication
  • Access Control: Only you can access your account and data
  • No Sharing: We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing

4.2 Data Breach

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Inform relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate action to secure the system
  • Provide guidance on protective measures

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Access all your stored analyses in the Account tab
  • Delete Analyses: Remove individual outfit analyses anytime
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and all data (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data (contact support)

5.2 Privacy Preferences

  • Guest Mode: Use the app without creating an account (data stored locally only)
  • Cloud Sync: Optional - only if you create an account
  • Photo Permissions: You control camera and photo library access

5.3 Marketing Communications

  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from optional communications anytime
  • Essential Emails: We may still send account-related notifications (password reset, etc.)

6. Children's Privacy

  • Age Requirement: NUUM is not intended for children under 13
  • Parental Consent: Users under 18 should have parental consent
  • Data Protection: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Removal: If we learn we've collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately

7. International Users

7.1 Data Transfer

  • Global Service: NUUM is available worldwide
  • Data Location: Data may be stored on servers in different countries
  • Compliance: We comply with applicable international privacy laws

7.2 GDPR (European Users)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Right to Restrict: Request restriction of processing

7.3 CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

8. Cookies and Tracking

8.1 Mobile App

  • No Web Cookies: As a native iOS app, we don't use browser cookies
  • Local Storage: We store preferences locally on your device
  • Analytics: We may use anonymized analytics (can be disabled in iOS Settings)

8.2 Do Not Track

  • We respect iOS privacy settings
  • You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking

9. Data Retention

9.1 Active Accounts

  • Personal Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Analysis History: Stored until you delete it
  • Photos: Stored until you delete them

9.2 Deleted Accounts

  • Immediate Deletion: Account data deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: May remain in backups for up to 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained if legally required

9.3 Guest Users

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app
  • No Cloud Backup: Data not synced to our servers

10. Changes to This Policy

  • Updates: We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices
  • Notification: Significant changes will be announced in the app
  • Effective Date: Changes effective upon posting
  • Review: Check this page periodically for updates
