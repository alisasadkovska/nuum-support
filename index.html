Frequently Asked Questions

How do I analyze an outfit?

1. Tap "Pick Photo" or "Take Photo"
2. Select your outfit photo
3. Tap "Analyze"
4. View your results!

How many free analyses do I get?

You get 3 free outfit analyses every day. For unlimited analyses, upgrade to NUUM Pro.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Tap the Pro badge in the Account tab to see subscription options.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the subscription screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

Can I delete my data?

Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account" to remove all your data.
